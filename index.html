Ryan Gosling Movies

Reviews and Summaries

La La Land (2016)

Director: Damien Chazelle Rating: 8.0/10

Summary:

A jazz pianist falls for an aspiring actress in Los Angeles as they both pursue their dreams in the city known for crushing hopes and breaking hearts.

Review:

"La La Land is a dazzling musical with captivating performances from Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone. The film's nostalgic charm and innovative cinematography make it a modern classic."

Drive (2011)

Director: Nicolas Winding Refn Rating: 7.8/10

Summary:

A mysterious Hollywood stuntman and mechanic moonlights as a getaway driver and finds himself in trouble when he helps out his neighbor.

Review:

"Drive is a stylish and intense thriller that showcases Ryan Gosling's ability to convey emotion with minimal dialogue. The film's neon-soaked atmosphere and pulsating soundtrack create a uniquely immersive experience."

The Notebook (2004)

Director: Nick Cassavetes Rating: 7.8/10

Summary:

A poor yet passionate young man falls in love with a rich young woman, giving her a sense of freedom, but they are soon separated because of their social differences.

Review:

"The Notebook is a heartwarming romantic drama that launched Ryan Gosling into stardom. His chemistry with Rachel McAdams is palpable, making this film a beloved classic in the romance genre."
